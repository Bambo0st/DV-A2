Total Cases per million

This plot shows the total number of cases, per million of population, recorded in a country at the beginning of each month in 2021. As expected, the number increases with time in every coutry.

Stringency Index

The plot shows the value of stringency index at the start of every month in 2021. Stringency index is a "composite measure based on 9 response indicators including school closures, workplace closures, and travel bans, rescaled to a value from 0 to 100". Note that the axes are scaled from 20-85 to improve readability.

Monthly Vaccinations per hundred

The plot shows the total number of vaccinations administered every month in 2021.
